Guitar Ear Training Games

FretBored

Wordle meets guitar. Guess notes by ear in Shredle, play back riffs on your real guitar, and level up with 55+ lessons. No ads, no accounts.

How You Play

With Your Guitar

  • Free Play — Hear a sequence, play it back. Difficulty adapts to your accuracy.
  • Curriculum — 12 modules, 55+ lessons from open strings to full-neck fluency.
  • Scale Practice — 20+ scales in open and higher positions.
  • Lick Library — 16 real phrases across blues, rock, folk, and jazz.
  • Interval Training — Hear two notes, name the distance. Three levels.
  • Tuner — Built-in chromatic tuner with cents display.

Without Your Guitar

  • Shredle — Wordle meets the fretboard. Guess a note sequence by ear.
  • Riff Memory — Simon Says, but for guitar riffs.
  • Scale Quiz — Hear a scale, name it.
  • Interval Quiz — Hear an interval, name it.
  • Scale Builder — Tap out the right notes to build a scale from scratch.

Your Privacy

No Accounts. No Tracking. No Ads.

FretBored works entirely offline. Your microphone is used only for real-time pitch detection—audio is never recorded or transmitted. Your data stays on your device.
