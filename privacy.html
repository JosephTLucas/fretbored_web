Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Overview

FretBored is a guitar ear-training app that runs entirely on your device. We are committed to your privacy and designed the app so that your data stays with you.

Data Collection

FretBored does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app makes no network requests and has no server-side component.

Microphone Access

FretBored requests microphone access to detect the notes you play on your guitar. Audio is processed in real time on your device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Local Data Storage

Your game progress, scores, practice history, and preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData and UserDefaults frameworks. This data never leaves your device.

Notifications

FretBored may request permission to send local notifications for practice reminders. These are scheduled entirely on-device. No push notification service or remote server is used.

Third-Party Services

FretBored uses no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no third-party SDKs in the app.

Children's Privacy

FretBored does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the revised version here with a new "Last updated" date.
